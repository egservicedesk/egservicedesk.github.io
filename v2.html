📌Reminders📌

FSS handles Pump & Card Reader Hardware Issues
CF IT Techs (FTS) do NOT handle any physical hardware issues with pumps or dispensers, including:
  • Card readers not working
  • Errors on card reader screens
  • Pumps not dispensing fuel
These issues should be escalated directly to Gas Maintenance (FSS), provided KBs for EO1 error / Avalan have already been followed.
Fuel Spill Handling
As the Help Desk team covers after hours for FSS, please ensure the following process is followed for fuel spill calls:
  1. Transfer the call to the SPILL LINE (1-800-225-9702 x4494) before completing the service channel ticket.
  2. Once the category falls under the FSS team, the ticket automatically routes to their queue. Transferring the call first ensures proper handling.
  3. Log a note on the ticket that the call was properly transferred to the spill line.
  4. Post confirmation in the FSS Zoom channel for visibility.

Reinforce After-Hours Support Process
Down Store Incidents:
Store Escalations (After Hours)
Helpdesk L2s/Leads will post the issue in the (EGA) MS Teams Channel.
  • Make multiple call attempts if the first one fails.
  • Leave a voicemail on the first attempt to ensure the on-call contact is informed.
  • Use the correct contact number (mobile) instead of Zoom to avoid missed connections.
  • All tickets must also be posted to both Facilities and FTS Zoom channels.
FTS (After Hours)
  • If confirmed down store and requires hardware replacement or on-site support, contact on-call FTS immediately.
  • If the on-call contact is unreachable after the 3rd attempt, escalate to John Wingo – details available in the FTS Zoom channel.
Facilities (After Hours)
  • Call the on-call gas technicians listed in the GC posted by George.
  • If unreachable, and for other issues aside from gas-related concerns, contact Alicia and Jonathan using their mobile numbers (available in the KB).
MIM Incidents
  • Identify and Confirm the Issue
  • Engage MIMs Immediately (within 30mins)
  • Initiate a call to the Major Incident Management (MIM) team.
  • Major Incident - SWAT and Problem Process
  • Activate MOTD for AWS/Kaseya to manage call volumes.
  • AWS MOTD Creation process
  • Send Kaseya Notification
  • Issue mass communication via Kaseya to notify affected users.
  • Sending Kaseya Message
  • Inform Outage GC Channel in (ACN) MS Teams
  • Post outage update with:
    • Affected system
    • Impact summary
    • Actions taken so far
    • ETA if known
  • Notify Store Escalation Teams/Zoom Helpdesk channel
  • Share update in Teams Store Escalation Channel for awareness.
  • Ongoing Monitoring & Documentation
  • Monitor system status and user impact continuously.
  • Document:
    • Timeline of events
    • Actions taken
    • All communications and updates
  • Maintain a running log until resolution is confirmed.

Virtualized Support Process

  1. If the store ticket is tagged as DOWNSTORE and it has been 1–5 days since the virtualization date, → Contact the on-call virtualization resource for immediate assistance.
    Please call Nelson Garcia — (508) 270-5808, (774) 386-1250
  2. For general issues, → The store should submit an SSP ticket or call the Helpdesk (Option 4)
  3. The Helpdesk will:
    • Identify the issue.
    • Gather necessary details from the store.
    • Submit a Smartsheet form to the project team for resolution.
    → Bug & Issues Log Submission Form Link:
    https://app.smartsheet.com/b/form/c72a0e3154e54aadb1cb623e5462fea2

  5. Helpdesk team uses the Bug & Issues tracker to monitor progress.
    → Bug & Issues Log for Monitoring:
    https://app.smartsheet.com/sheets/jV56GgpGHq6mJmCHPC3xqQxpV5mFWW2M7mfmMrH1?view=grid
